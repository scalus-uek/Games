# League of Legends

## Overview
League of Legends (LoL) is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Riot Games.
The game was released in 2009 and quickly became one of the most played PC games in the world.

## Gameplay
Players compete in two teams of five, each controlling a unique champion with special abilities.
The main objective is to destroy the enemy Nexus while defending your own base.

## Popularity and Esports
League of Legends is a global esport with professional leagues, international tournaments,
and millions of viewers worldwide. The annual World Championship is one of the biggest esports events.

## Champions and Strategy
There are over 160 champions in the game, each with different roles such as mage, tank, assassin,
marksman, and support. Strategy and teamwork are key to winning matches.

## Community and Updates
Riot Games regularly updates the game with new champions, balance changes, and events.
The community is very active, creating guides, videos, and fan content.
