League of Legends

Overview

League of Legends (LoL) is a multiplayer online battle arena game developed by Riot Games.

Gameplay

Two teams of five players compete to destroy the enemy Nexus using unique champions.

Popularity and Esports

LoL is one of the biggest esports titles in the world with global tournaments.

Champions and Strategy

The game features over 160 champions and requires teamwork and strategy.

Community and Updates

The game is regularly updated with new content and balance changes.
